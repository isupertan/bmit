Our Company History

Having learnt the technical know-how, Mr. Ramphool Jaiswaal. Setup Bearing Machinotech (INDIA) Kolkata, West Bengal in 1975. At the present scenario Bearing Machinotech has spreaded its wings over India as well as Abroad. BMTI has become a household name of Heavy Earthmoving section from dealer to users, we need no introduction, we are certified importer and exporter. We have a warehouse where almost every type of Bearings are kept in stock. We deal with NTN, SKF, KoYo, Nachi, INA, BCA, BowER, NSK, and other complicated heavy Bearings of the USA. We specialized in Loader, Dozer, Dumper, Excavator, Crain, Vibrating Road Roller, Shovel, and All Bearing available as per part number. Our specialized field is coal mines, cement plants, and other Industries. We are a proud member of ALL India Ball Bearing Merchants Association and Eastern India Ball Bearing Merchant Association. Mr. Shailendra Jaiswal after completing a Master's degree in Marketing Management joined in 2005. We are associated with various NGO and social organizations to-do needful philanthropic activities towards society when needed like blood donation camps and water projects and child care.
